The Story So Far

I'm Yogesh Century B.K., an iOS App Engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal — but my journey started long before the capital city.

Right after finishing high school (+2 level), I dove straight into the tech world — repairing computers, printers, and managing system networks. That hands-on grounding gave me something most engineers don't have: a deep intuition for how machines actually work.

I then pursued a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering from Lumbini Engineering College. In my 3rd year, I was already giving back — teaching Computer Science and Mathematics to students in Classes 8–10.

After graduating, I moved to Kathmandu with one goal: build real software. I landed an iOS internship at QPay Private Limited, then leveled up to a full iOS Engineer role at Pathway Technologies, where I currently craft mobile experiences used by real people across Nepal and beyond.
